Layout Guard

Wrong keyboard layout warning settings. Detection runs locally in your browser.

Typed in the wrong layout? If your text looks like a real word in another layout, Layout Guard gives you a quick sound reminder. It listens only in fields on web pages, not in the browser address bar, New Tab search, or Ctrl+F. To avoid bothering you, it skips short, random, or already valid words.
